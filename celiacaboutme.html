About Me

My name is Avery, I'm a junior in high school and I was diagnosed with Celiac Disease when I was 8 years old.


Ever since I was diagnosed, I wanted to help others with the taxing disease. Oftentimes, after diagnosis and following a gluten free diet, people feel drastically better physically. What many don't consider is how they are doing mentally.
