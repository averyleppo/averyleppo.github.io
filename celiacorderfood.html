Tips for Ordering Food

Ordering food with celiac disease can sometimes be a stressful experience. If you are at a resturant with your friends and the resturant isn't fully gluten free, you can maybe feel nervous to order. This feeling makes sense. So hopefully, these tips will help alleviate the stress.

Something you can do before you even leave your house, or get to the resturant is creating a little script for yourself. It helps you say all the important points you need to say without the stress of doing it all improvised.
