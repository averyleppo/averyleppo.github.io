Avery Leppo (me) created this camp. I am 16 years old and a rising 11th grader at The Dalton School. I live in NYC, but I have had a house in Washington, CT for the past 9 years. I have been involved in the Washington community and have made many close friends. I will be the main coach for the kids, but some of my talented and kind athletic friends will be other coaches.

I have played a number of sports over the years and am very qualified to begin coaching. Some of my athletic qualifications include: being a starter on the Dalton Varsity Softball Team since 9th grade, working at a kids basketball program, Dribbl, during the school year, I played soccer 5 years on the Shepaug recreational team.

I absolutely love working with kids. Over the years I have volunteered at my preschool, babysat, worked at Dribbl (basketball program), volunteered with ASAP! and worked at Eagle Rock Day Camp.
